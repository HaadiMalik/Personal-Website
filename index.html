About Me

Hello, my name is Haadi Malik. I graduated in August of 2024 with a Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science from The College of New Jersey (TCNJ). I graduated am now searching for a job in the industry while also keeping an open-mind about enrolling in a master's degree. Before deciding to develop this website, I was finishing college while also learning skills on my own through various websites and online courses. After a while, I realized that learning all these skills would be greatly helpful, but I needed to do more than "theoretical learning." To that end, I started attempting more practical projects, courses, problems, and hands-on experience.

While I am refining my skills and learning new concepts, my personal life has not come to a stop.
